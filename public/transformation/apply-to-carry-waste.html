This page will keep you up to date on the progress of the 7th exemplar in our Transformation programme – Waste carrier registration.

Right now it contains our original strategy statement for the exemplar, the scope we've developed during the discovery phase of the programme, a quick description of the benefits we aim to deliver to users and other useful background information – as available.

As the programme continues you'll be able to see progress reflected here and the information will become richer – there'll be links to the programme blog, screenshots of software in development and more and better data as we learn more about the delivery of the service.

Digital strategy statement

Key benefits of service redesign will include:
  • Provision of a user-friendly and convenient digital service channel.
  • Reducing the costs associated with the current paper-based system for business and government.
  • Potentially integrating with other business-focused transactions across government

From the Department for Environment Food & Rural Affairs digital strategy – December 2012

Benefits for users

If you need to register to handle waste, we'll make it quick and easy online

Scope of exemplar

The new service will enable easy online registration as a waste carrier. Savings currently forecast at between £2m and £4m, to be achieved over the next five years

7
