  1. Digital services, not websites

    Our primary concern - everyone who needs and wants to use our services finds it as easy as possible to do so. We should not be afraid to make things obvious. We should not redesign common web practises that already work.

    Find out what people are actually doing. The benefit of digital services - we can actually test with real users.

  5. Do the work to make it simple

    Do all the hard work to make it look simple for the user. Making things simple for the user will make them hard for us. Albert Einstein's maxim "everything should be made as simple as possible, but no simpler"
